eAbsentee

eAbsentee is an online absentee ballot request platform. it's built on a super simple Flask server with no hidden magic. it collects user data to help canvassers know what canvassing methods are working.

in the 2019 local election cycle, eAbsentee helped 718 people apply for and receive absentee ballots. key metrics include applications from 76 counties, over 25 campaigns worked with, in around 5 weeks.

check it out at eAbsentee.org
